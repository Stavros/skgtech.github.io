About

We are Together is an afternoon gathering where local techies come together present their products, listen to fresh ideas and network. The event is admission free and open to anyone interested into new technologies and business creation. The afternoon starts with the Startup Track and ends with a themed panel discussion "AI & the Bots". Between those two sessions we have some open time-slots. The story

The "We Are Together" event has been held 2 times (April & November 2014) and has been the springboard for developing the local technology community and creating meetup teams. The theme of the two previous events was the community itself, where all the technology groups of the city and those who wanted to create new teams had the opportunity to talk and make their efforts known to an audience of more than 150 people that were present. The 3rd event of the series takes current needs into consideration and adapts its theme featuring tech startup companies and their efforts to get into the global market. The event is organized by SKGTech a non profit organization comprised of creative and passionate people that love all things tech.
