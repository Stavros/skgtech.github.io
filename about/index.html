About SKGTech

who we are

We are SKGTech.

SKGTech is a non profit organization based in Thessaloniki, Greece. The organization is comprised of creative and passionate people that love all the things revolving around technology.

We organize local meetups, international conferences and create apps and services to aid our society at large. SKGTech is all about people; networking and bringing the people with common interests together to create synergies.

You can read SKGTech Bylaws here

history

2014

  • SKGTech is founded
  • The two "We Are Together" events take place and ignite the local tech communities

2015

  • SKGTech incorporates as a Non Profit Entity
  • The first DEVit is organized, 350 people participated
  • The second version of skgtech.io website launches

2016

  • The second DEVit is organized, 420 people participated
  • The third version of skgtech.io website launches
vision

To create a thriving ecosystem where all can benefit by empowering and uniting the technological communities of Thessaloniki

mission

SKGTech embraces & brings together all the people who love technology with the ultimate goal to inspire, to create and to serve its members and the society they live in.
